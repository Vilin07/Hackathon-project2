📦 Sell Online - Simple Guides

🛍 Meesho

Step 1: Download the Meesho app from Play Store.

Step 2: Sign up with your mobile number.

Step 3: Go to "My Shop" → Add Product.

Step 4: Upload product photos, set price, and add a description.

Step 5: Share your products on WhatsApp or Facebook!

📘 Watch Meesho Tutorial

🛒 Amazon Saheli

Step 1: Visit Amazon Saheli.

Step 2: Register as a woman entrepreneur (you may need NGO verification).

Step 3: Create a seller account and list your products.

Step 4: Amazon handles shipping and payments.

📘 Watch Amazon Saheli Guide

📸 Instagram

Step 1: Create a free Instagram Business account.

Step 2: Post clear photos/videos of your products.

Step 3: Use hashtags like #handmade, #tailoring, #womenentrepreneurs.

Step 4: Add price and contact info in the caption or bio.

📘 Watch Instagram Selling Tips

💬 WhatsApp

Step 1: Use WhatsApp Business app for free.

Step 2: Set a business profile with address and category.

Step 3: Add your products in the “Catalog” section.

Step 4: Share your catalog with customers directly.

📘 Watch WhatsApp Catalog Tutorial

🌟 Tip: Start with one platform and grow gradually!
