About Digital Skills & Career Building

Digital Skills & Career Building focuses on providing rural girls and women with the essential tools needed to thrive in today's digital world. This module covers foundational digital literacy, including the basics of computers, smartphones, online communication, and internet safety. It introduces coding through beginner-friendly languages and platforms, enabling learners to create and innovate through hands-on digital projects. Additionally, it emphasizes practical career skills such as resume building, interview preparation, and access to scholarship and exam resources. Learners are also guided toward free, recognized certificate courses from platforms like Google, Coursera, and edX. By combining tech skills with career development, this module empowers participants to enhance their job readiness, unlock new career opportunities, explore entrepreneurship, and gain confidence in their ability to succeed in an ever-evolving digital economy.
